Campus Life

School church

Was established in 1978

St. Xavier's Collegiate School (informally SXCS) is a private Catholic primary and secondary school for boys, located in Kolkata, West Bengal, India.



school library

Was build in 1980

A well stocked library is open to the pupils on all class days. It is also supplied with periodicals and magazines and is a veritable treasure house of reference books and encyclopedias. Each class is given a library period where the students are to read the books and make review of them.



school auditorium

Was build in 1982

The Millennium Complex, constructed at the turn of the new millennium, is ultra modern, with facilities and amenities that go even beyond our four decade dream.
